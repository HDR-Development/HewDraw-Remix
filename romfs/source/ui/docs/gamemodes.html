Tag, you're it!
In this custom game mode, a random player starts with the final smash aura! Hit other players to pass it on.
Once time runs out, you die!

Do you have what it takes to outlast your opponents?

Turbo mode!
In this custom game mode, players can immediately cancel any attack that hits their opponent.
Break your fingers while touch-of-deathing your friends!

Hitfall mode!
In this custom game mode, players can immediately fastfall during hitlag, even if you are rising.
Make some cool combos using forced fastfalls!

Airdash mode!
In this custom game mode, players can immediately cancel their airdodge, keeping the momentum.
This behavior is inspired by certain fighting games, as well as Project NX.

Clash in classic fashion!
Removes DI, airdodges, walltechs, and landing lag. Also raises hitstun and shieldstun and alters character physics.

Play the game!
WARNING: When playing online, desyncs will occur if these settings are not identical between players!
